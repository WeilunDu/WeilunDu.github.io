Welcome to Tufts Biochemistry Flashcards Support page.

Why spend time making your own flashcards when you can download these course-specific decks in under a minute? The cards in this app will help you prepare for the medical biochemistry portion of the exams for medical foundations 1 at Tufts University School of Medicine. 

 swipe upward/downward to check out the answer for questions
 tap the star in the right corner to bookmark down your question
 go to bookmark section to review

But, still...

This mobile application was created to help you study for the medical biochemistry course. It is in no way intended to serve as or replace your primary source of studying material such as your syllabus and lecture slides. Should you have any difficulty understanding the material, please contact the appropriate lecturer or your course director.
